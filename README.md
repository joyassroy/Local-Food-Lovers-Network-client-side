My Apps Name: The Town Munchies


Features are:


Share Detailed Food Reviews: Users can post their own food experiences, including a food name, image, restaurant name, location, star rating, and detailed review text .


Discover Local Favorites: Browse all reviews from the community, sorted by the most recently added , or see top-rated featured reviews on the homepage.



Full Review Management: Logged-in users can manage their own submissions on a "My Reviews" page, with options to Edit or Delete their posts.




Database-Powered Search: A powerful search bar on the "All Reviews" page allows users to find reviews by food name, using MongoDB's $regex operator for server-side searching.


Secure Authentication: Users can securely register and log in using either their email and password or their Google account, all handled by Firebase Authentication.
